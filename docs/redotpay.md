# RedotPay — Paying for AI with Crypto

[RedotPay](https://www.redotpay.com) is a crypto payment platform that lets you pay for AI API subscriptions using cryptocurrency. It's the cheapest way to bridge crypto → fiat for developer tools.

---

## What RedotPay Is

A platform offering:
- **Virtual & physical Visa cards** — spend crypto anywhere Visa is accepted
- **Multi-currency wallets** — hold USDC, USDT, BTC, ETH, and more
- **P2P transfers** — send to other RedotPay users for free
- **Crypto swaps** — convert between currencies at market rates

**Key stats**: 7M+ users, 100+ countries, $10B+ annual payment volume.

---

## Why Use It for AI APIs

### The Problem
International developers often face:
- High FX fees when paying USD for API subscriptions
- Limited banking access to international merchants
- Slow settlement times for cross-border payments

### The Solution
```
Crypto wallet  →  RedotPay card  →  OpenAI / Anthropic / GitHub
   (USDC)          (Visa, free)      (pay like any card)
```

**Savings**: Avoid 2–5% FX fees. Pay directly in stablecoins.

## Setup (5 minutes)

### 1. Create an Account

Download the RedotPay app (iOS/Android) or sign up at [redotpay.com](https://www.redotpay.com).

### 2. Get Verified (KYC)

Basic KYC required for virtual cards. Usually approved within minutes.

### 3. Create a Virtual Card

- Virtual cards are **free**
- Physical cards available for ~$100 (optional)
- Instant activation

### 4. Load Your Card

Deposit crypto to your RedotPay wallet:
- Supported: USDC, USDT, BTC, ETH, SOL, and more
- USDC/USDT recommended (stable value, no price fluctuation)
- Minimum deposit varies by currency

### 5. Use It

Add your RedotPay card to:
- **OpenAI** (ChatGPT Plus, API)
- **Anthropic** (Claude API)
- **GitHub** (Copilot)
- **Google** (Gemini API, Google Cloud)
- Any other service that accepts Visa

## Pro Tips

1. **Use USDC or USDT** — avoid BTC/ETH for payments (price volatility)
2. **Load in bulk** — top up once a month to minimize deposit fees
3. **Virtual card only** — you don't need the physical card for online subscriptions
4. **Set spending limits** — cap your card at your monthly AI budget
5. **Keep a backup card** — if one gets declined, have a second virtual card ready
6. **Monitor exchange rates** — if loading with non-USD crypto, time your deposits
